Tribute to Julie Andrews

Biography

Julie Andrews full name is Julia Elizabeth Andrews. She was born in Walton-on-Thames, England on October 1st in 1935. She came from a musical famly and started her acting debut in the 1940s before she moved to America. Andrews began to star in musicals and became well known for that. Around 1964, she became more popular than ever from starring in Mary Poppins and the Sound of Music. In 1997, Andrews had a large setback that many would not have imagine would happen. Somehow her vocal chords were damaged during a operation. Julie Andrews wasn't able to fully regain her strong singing voice, but she kept singing beautifully. Today, she still acts and does voice acting. She has been acting for roughly seventy-nine years and has created an amazing impact on all who have seen or heard her act.
